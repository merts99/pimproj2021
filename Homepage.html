Welcome to HISTO! The application for Dutch high school history students! You can use this app to do our Rijksmuseum quiz! Also, if it is not possible to visit our museum, you can have a look in our virtual rooms! Here you can view prominent paintings of Rembrandt van Rijn, Frans Hals, and Vincent van Gogh. History is a fun yet hard subject for high school students. Do you think you can come up with a difficult skull cracking question? Send us your question and maybe you'll win our Rijksmuseum reward!
